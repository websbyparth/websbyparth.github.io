Our Story

A café born
under the moon's
gentle glow

Tucked on the first floor of AR Mall, The Moon Café is Surat's most atmospheric escape. We built this place for the night owls, the coffee purists, the ones who need a quiet corner to think — and the ones who just want to feel something beautiful.

From precision-brewed single origins to indulgent Biscoff cheesecakes, everything here is made with the kind of care that only a moon-lit night can inspire.

Why Come Here

The Experience

Moon-Themed Ambiance
Every detail — from the neon MOON sign to the pendant lights — is designed to transport you into an otherworldly calm. It's not just a café, it's an atmosphere.
Coffee Craftsmanship
Six manual brew methods. Signature cold brews aged in rum and whiskey barrels. Elevated espresso tonics. This is coffee for people who care about what's in their cup.
Open Until 1 AM
The city sleeps. The Moon doesn't. Whether it's a midnight study session, a late-night conversation, or just you and your thoughts — we're here for it.
Artisan Cheesecakes
"Simply amazing — rich, creamy, absolutely delicious." From classic New York to indulgent Double Decker, our cheesecakes are the reason people come back.
